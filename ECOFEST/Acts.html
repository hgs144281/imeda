Ecofest

Acts

The 4 main acts coming to Ecofest all care about our environment and our world as much as the folks behind Ecofest

Everyone's Environment - A four piece indie band local to Harrogate who use their influence of beautiful music to raise money for the environment

 

Solar Drum - A pop band who centre around the problems caused by the sun and the wonders it creates

 

Green Gizmos - A six piece band who use puppets to teach children about the troubles the environment is facing

 

DJ John Alfred - The legendary local DJ. The Beast.
